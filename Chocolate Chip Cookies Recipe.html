Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Salted butter, melted
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all purpose flavour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet Chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Cream together 1 cup of softened butter, 1 cup of white sugar, and 1 cup of brown sugar in a large bowl until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in 2 large eggs, one at a time. Then add 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract and mix well.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together 3 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of baking soda, ½ teaspoon of baking powder, and ½ teaspoon of salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until a soft dough forms.
  6. Fold in 2 cups of chocolate chips
  7. Scoop tablespoon-sized balls of dough and place them on the baking tray, leaving space between each cookie.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 12 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
  9. Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking tray for 5 minutes.
  10. Transfer the cookies to a wire rack to cool completely before serving.

Notes

For softer cookies, take them out of the oven when they still look slightly underdone in the center. They will continue to cook while cooling on the tray.
